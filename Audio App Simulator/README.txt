Audio App Simulator

Description:


The Audio App Simulator is a project written entirely in Java, similar to Apple Music. It makes use of object-oriented programming, arrays, arrayLists, exceptions, polymorphism, collections (maps), inheritance, and interfaces to store and manage types of audio content, including Song and AudioBook. Users are able to see and store content, download songs and audiobooks, create new playlists, add and delete content from playlists and much more! Feel free to dive in and create your own playlists!
